About MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology)

MIT, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, is a world-renowned institution at the forefront of scientific and technological innovation. Established in 1861, MIT is dedicated to advancing knowledge and educating students in the fields of science, engineering, economics, and the social sciences. Our mission is to address complex global challenges through cutting-edge research and education, while instilling a sense of curiosity and a commitment to excellence in our students.
